SUMMARY

  • Dr. Bing Sun is on the verge of becoming a world-class researcher in the area of microstructured optical fibres (MOFs). Even at this relatively early stage of his career he has an internationally recognized track record of publications in the fields of numerical modeling of MOFs, optical fibre devices and optical fibre sensors. He possesses an excellent combination of theoretical and experimental expertise in the areas of MOFs infiltration of functional materials. His PhD thesis is focused on the design of coupled devices based on photonic crystal fibres and optical waveguides. In July 2013, his dissertation was awarded the prestigious “Excellent Doctoral Dissertation Prize at Jiangsu University in 2013”. As a Post-doctoral research fellow at Shenzhen University, he experimentally developed infiltrated MOFs for application in optical fibre sensors. In addition, he possesses a deep expertise in mode coupling analyses and bandgap calculations of MOFs infiltration of functional materials, which has required a solid knowledge in mathematical and theoretical physics. Furthermore, essential elements of his research experience have been the enthusiasm for learning new things, creativity, and the ability to work independently and to collaborate with work colleagues.
